I hold a Bachelor of Arts degree specializing in Geography and Anthropology
from the University of Johannesburg. I have also completed

I'm a passionate and proactive Data Analyst with a strong foundation in Geography and Anthropology. While my academic journey began with these disciplines, my curiosity led me down an exciting path into the world of data analysis. As a young scholar, I immersed myself in geography and anthropology, captivated by the art of mapmaking and captivated by the potential of data visualization. It was during this journey that I stumbled upon the realm of "Data Analytics." The allure of transforming raw data into meaningful insights became a driving force for me.

Adapting to change is essential, and I found myself embracing new experiences and challenges. While completing my final year of studies, I embraced a role as a cashier—a surprising choice for an introvert. But this role taught me vital communication skills, preparing me for the collaborative world of data analysis. Graduating was a crowning achievement, but my journey was far from over.

Unlocking the Power of Data Analytics

In the realm of data analytics, every dataset is a potential story waiting to be told. My journey into this world began with two transformative virtual internships—one with KPMG and the other with Accenture. These experiences exposed me to the intricacies of data cleaning, wrangling, and visualization. Armed with Microsoft Excel, I embarked on a journey to uncover insights hidden within sprawling datasets.

My toolbox of skills expanded rapidly. I learned to wield SQL to seamlessly navigate databases, extracting valuable insights with each query. Microsoft Excel, once a familiar tool, transformed into a canvas for data analysis masterpieces—VLOOKUP and Pivot Tables became my companions on this journey. The magic of data visualization unfolded as I delved into Tableau. This tool transformed numbers into stories, revealing trends and patterns with captivating visuals. I honed my skills in data storytelling, turning complex insights into narratives that anyone could understand.

The Path Forward
